# 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

最后更新日期：2026-02-14 / Last Updated: 2026-02-14

## 简介
本隐私政策说明我们如何在用户使用《LLM-Shark》桌面应用时处理信息。我们重视用户隐私并遵守适用法律法规。

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the “LLM-Shark” desktop app. We value your privacy and comply with applicable laws.

## 适用范围
本政策适用于《LLM-Shark》Windows 桌面应用及与之相关的商店服务。

## Scope
This Policy applies to the “LLM-Shark” Windows desktop app and related store services.

## 我们处理的信息
- 本地文件与内容：您选择的 PCAP 文件、解析结果与报告内容仅在本地设备上处理与存储
- 配置与偏好：模型端点地址、tshark 路径、语言与主题等设置仅保存在本地
- 商店订阅与交易信息：用于授权校验的产品 ID、订阅状态、到期时间等（不包含完整支付卡号、CVV）
- 本地诊断日志：可能在本地生成用于排障的日志或请求记录，便于问题定位

## Information We Handle
- Local files and content: PCAP files, analysis results, and reports are processed and stored locally
- Settings and preferences: model endpoint, tshark path, language, theme, stored locally
- Store subscriptions and transactions: product ID, entitlement status, expiration for license checks (no full card numbers, CVV)
- Local diagnostics: logs or request records may be generated locally for troubleshooting

## 信息来源
- 您在应用中的操作与设置
- Microsoft Store 提供的订阅状态与交易回执字段

## Sources of Information
- Your in-app actions and settings
- Subscription status and receipt fields provided by Microsoft Store

## 我们如何使用信息
- 提供与运行核心功能：文件解析、报告生成、订阅权益校验
- 故障排查与性能优化：在本地查看日志以定位问题
- 安全与合规：防止滥用与满足合规要求

## How We Use Information
- Provide and run core features: parsing, reporting, entitlement checks
- Troubleshooting and performance: review local logs to diagnose issues
- Security and compliance: prevent abuse and meet legal obligations

## 数据存储与保留
- 主要数据均保存在本地设备
- 订阅缓存与授权相关信息以最小化方式保存，并在本地加密
- 日志与临时文件可由用户自行删除

## Storage and Retention
- Data is primarily stored on your local device
- Subscription cache and entitlement data are stored minimally and encrypted locally
- Logs and temporary files can be deleted by the user

## 数据共享与传输
- 不出售用户信息
- 仅在以下情况下会发生网络传输：
  - Microsoft Store 授权与订阅校验
  - 您自行配置的模型端点：当您启用 LLM 功能时，报告内容与提示会发送到该端点
- 法律与监管要求：在法律要求情况下披露

## Sharing and Transfers
- We do not sell user information
- Network transfers occur only for:
  - Microsoft Store entitlement and subscription validation
  - Your configured model endpoint when LLM features are enabled
- Legal and regulatory requests as required

## 儿童隐私
本应用面向普通用户；若您是未成年人，请在监护人指导下使用。我们不会主动收集儿童个人信息。

## Children’s Privacy
The app targets general audiences; if you are a minor, please use under guardian guidance. We do not knowingly collect children’s personal data.

## 安全

## Security

## Your Rights
